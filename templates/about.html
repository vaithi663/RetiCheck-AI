Frequently Asked Questions

Is this system meant to replace doctors?

Absolutely not. RetiCheck AI is a Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) designed to assist ophthalmologists, not replace their clinical judgment.

How is patient data secured?

We use localized SQLite processing and environment-level protections. No unauthorized personnel can access the patient database without the Admin PIN.

What is a Grad-CAM Heatmap?

It is an Explainable AI (XAI) feature that visually highlights the specific areas of the retina that caused the AI to predict a disease, adding a layer of trust.

Can patients upload smartphone photos?

No. For accurate clinical predictions, the system requires standard fundus imagery taken by professional non-mydriatic retinal cameras.
