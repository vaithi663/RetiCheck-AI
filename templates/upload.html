Image Quality Guidelines

For optimal AI prediction accuracy, ensure the uploaded fundus images meet the clinical standards:

  • Macula centered with clear optic disc visibility.
  • Minimum resolution of 1024x1024 pixels.
  • Avoid severe glare or light artifacts.

Diagnostic Parameters

Our deep learning models currently evaluate the retinal scans for multiple pathological features:

  • Diabetic Retinopathy: Microaneurysms, Exudates.
  • Glaucoma: Cup-to-Disc ratio abnormalities.
  • Macular Edema: Fluid accumulation detection.

AI Analysis Methodology

RetiCheck employs a sophisticated Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) architecture. Post-processing involves Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) to produce visual heatmaps, ensuring clinical explainability.

Data Security & Compliance

All clinical data and patient images are processed temporarily in a secure buffer. Permanent records are stored locally with AES-256 encryption, adhering strictly to global HIPAA patient privacy protocols.
